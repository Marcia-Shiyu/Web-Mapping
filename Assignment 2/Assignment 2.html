Map 1: Washington DC Cherry Blossoms

Each spring, the Tidal Basin is surrounded by pink and white blooms, drawing visitors from all over the globe. The first time I explored the beauty of cherry blossoms in Washington DC was in 2022. I highly recommend this city if you want to see cherry blossom and you do not want to travel abroad.

Map 2: Wuhan Cherry Blossoms

Discover the vibrant cherry blossoms at Wuhan's East Lake. This scenic spot offers spectacular views of pink blossoms that perfectly reflect in the tranquil lake waters. I lived in Wuhan for four years and each spring my friends and I had picnic by the lake and enjoyed the blossom together.

Map 3: Tokyo Cherry Blossoms

Ueno Park comes alive with the bloom of thousands of cherry trees. People celebrate with festivals and picnics under the pink canopy. I hope that my family and I can see the beautiful scenery together some time next year.
